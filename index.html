Our glove families

Different applications, one consistent standard of quality. Explore the core Just 1 Source ranges below.

Predator by Ron®

The famous Predator by Ron® range is widely recognised in the industry; setting the benchmark for quality and consistency for leather work gloves. The Predator by Ron® range incorporates Rigger Gloves, Driver’s Gloves, MIG Welding Gauntlets, TIG Welding Gauntlets, and more.

Coloursafe

Our vast range of knitted, coated work gloves covers various industries and applications. We colour coordinate work gloves to assist in identifying users are wearing the correct level of safety for the task in hand – offering Waterproof protection and Touchscreen compatibility, to name a few benefits.

Watersafe

Our Watersafe range of waterproof gloves helps protect your hands in adverse weather and applications. These gloves will keep the hands dry when carrying out tasks where waterproof gloves are essential; offering superb suction grip. We also have thermal options available – the Pred Baltic and Pred Arctic.

Touchsafe

Our Touchsafe gloves are some of the most exciting in our portfolio, featuring patented technology and a revolutionary PolyMAXTM coating, to increase production, reduce downtime, offering cost-in-use benefits. Gloves within the Touchsafe range offer the maximum Cut Level F Protection, with an Impact work glove option available.
